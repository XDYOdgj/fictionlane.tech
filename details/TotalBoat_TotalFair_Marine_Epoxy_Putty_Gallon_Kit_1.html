TotalBoat TotalFair: Marine-Grade Epoxy Putty for Durable Boat and Watercraft Repairs

TotalBoat TotalFair is a high-strength, lightweight two-part epoxy putty engineered specifically for marine environments. It's designed to deliver flawless, long-lasting repairs on boats, canoes, RVs, and vehicles. With its powerful bonding properties, this compound adheres firmly to wood, aluminum, steel, and fiberglass, making it a versatile solution for both below and above waterline fixes.

Whether you're tackling dents, holes, or gouges, TotalFair ensures a smooth, professional-grade finish that's ready to be sanded and painted in just a few hours. The improved formula features a simple 1:1 mix ratio, making application easier and more efficient. Its quick-drying nature prevents sagging on vertical or overhead surfaces, offering users a hassle-free experience for all repair projects.

Key Features

  • Durable, waterproof two-part epoxy filler with high strength and lightweight construction
  • Perfect for smoothing out rough spots and filling voids on watercraft and vehicles
  • Ideal for permanent repairs above and below the waterline
  • Foolproof 1:1 mix ratio with an improved formula for seamless application
  • Fast-curing compound that's ready to sand in as little as 4 hours

Why Choose TotalFair?

TotalFair is a must-have for anyone who wants to restore the beauty and integrity of their vessel with minimal effort. Its no-pinhole curing process creates a smooth finish that's ideal for painting, while the ease of mixing and spreading makes it perfect for both professionals and DIY enthusiasts. Whether you're working on a boat, RV, or car, TotalFair offers a reliable, high-performance solution that stands up to the toughest conditions.
