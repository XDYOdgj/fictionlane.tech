Serta Five Star Mattress

The Serta Five Star Mattress combines comfort and support to deliver a truly restful night's sleep. With 3 zoned support foam, it contours to your body and promotes optimal lower back alignment, ensuring you wake up refreshed and energized. Whether you're a light sleeper or enjoy sharing your bed with someone, this medium feel queen mattress is engineered for balanced support and pressure relief. The combination of PillowSoft Aire and PillowSoft foams makes it ideal for relieving discomfort in sensitive areas like your shoulders, hips, and knees.

For added comfort, the breathable cover fabric helps regulate temperature, keeping your sleep cool and comfortable throughout the night. Additionally, the CertiPUR-US certified mattress is free from harmful chemicals and gases, ensuring a safe and healthy sleep environment. The 10\" thickness offers the perfect balance of support and cushioning, making it a great choice for those who want a mattress that's both durable and comfortable.

Why Choose This Mattress?

Looking for a mattress that offers both comfort and support? The Serta Five Star Mattress is designed to provide just that. Its medium feel makes it perfect for people who prefer a balanced sleeping experience, and the 3 zoned support foam ensures that your body is properly aligned.
